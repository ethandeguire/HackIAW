FAQs


Do I have to know how to code?

NO! HackIAW is open to any high school student, even if you don't know what HTML is! Workshops on coding basics will be run throughout the day.

Do I have to come with a team?

No, while you can work alone if you really want, teams will be formed at the event.

Who is allowed to come?

Any high school student who can get to the event! If you're unsure about transportation, tell us when you sign up and we can help you look for a carpool.

How much does it cost?

HackIAW is completely free! Additionally, free food, stickers, and t-shirts will be given out at the event.

What will we be doing?

With your team, you will make a project of your design. The theme of HackIAW is Outer Space, but you're free to make anything you want.
